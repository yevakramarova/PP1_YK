- Peel the potatoes and rinse them under cold water.
Grate the potatoes using a box grater or a food processor. Place the grated potatoes in a bowl.
- Peel and finely chop the onion.
- Add the chopped onion to the grated potatoes.
Beat the eggs in a separate bowl, then add them to the potato and onion mixture.
Add flour, salt, and pepper to the mixture. Start with 2 tablespoons of flour and adjust as needed to achieve a thick batter consistency. Mix everything well.
- Heat vegetable oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
Once the oil is hot, spoon the potato mixture into the pan to form pancakes, using about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
Flatten the pancakes with the back of a spoon to ensure even cooking.
Fry the pancakes for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
Remove the pancakes from the pan and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil.
- Serve the Deruny hot as a side dish or appetizer.
You can serve them with sour cream, applesauce, or your favorite topping.
